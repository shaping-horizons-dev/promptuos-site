Features

Everything your agency needs

From client intake to payroll, Promptuos replaces the patchwork of tools holding your agency back.

📅

Smart Scheduling

Match providers to clients by availability, credentials, location, and authorized hours. Conflict detection and automated reminders reduce no-shows.

💰

Billing & Claims

Submit electronic claims, track authorizations, manage denials, and monitor revenue — all mapped to the correct CPT codes automatically.

💲

Payroll Integration

Session data flows directly into payroll. Support for multiple pay rates, contractor payments, and seamless integration with Gusto.

📋

Credential Tracking

Monitor RBT certifications, BCBA licensure, background checks, and CPR — with automated alerts before anything expires.

📊

Authorization Management

Track approved units per client and payer in real time. Get alerts when utilization is low or authorizations are nearing expiration.

📈

Analytics & Reporting

Utilization rates, cancellation trends, revenue per client, hours delivered vs. authorized — the KPIs that drive your business forward.
